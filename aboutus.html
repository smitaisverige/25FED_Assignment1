About Us

Meals on Wheels rolled into the heart of Stockholm with a mission to bring authentic Indian soul food to the streets — thoughtfully adapted to the Swedish palate. Founded by Chef Arjun Mehra, who brings over 20 years of experience from the kitchens of India’s top 5-star hotels, our food truck is where gourmet Indian cuisine meets everyday comfort, served with a side of Swedish warmth.

Our Vision:To become Stockholm's favorite destination for soulful Indian meals — served hot, fresh, and on-the-go.

Our Mission:To deliver chef-crafted Indian dishes with authentic flavor and Swedish sensibility, using fresh, locally sourced produce and ingredients.

Our Values:

  • Authenticity Meets Adaptability : Traditional Indian flavors, thoughtfully reimagined for Swedish tastes.
  • Craft & Comfort : Chef-driven dishes made with heart and heritage.
  • Local Love : We support Swedish farmers and producers through local sourcing.
  • Quality : Fresh ingredients, slow-cooked meals, fast service.

Bringing bold Indian flavors to the streets — with a modern twist!

With 20+ years in the food game, our founder 🧑‍🍳 blends Indian street classics with Nordic & Southern European vibes.
